Our company conducts not only system consulting for clients, but also upstream processes (System requirements definition, system concept, system planning) of systems by analysing business needs, and finally, develops software programs. We also operate the Project Management Office (PMO) directly or take charge of application development and perform system construction.

プロジェクトマネジメントオフィス（PMO）を直接運営したり、アプリケーション開発やシステム構築も行います。
